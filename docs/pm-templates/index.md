# PM checklist templates

Preventive maintenance checklist templates for common equipment types:

- [Compressor PM](./compressor-pm-checklist)
- [Excavator PM](./excavator-pm-checklist)
- [Forklift PM](./forklift-pm-checklist)
- [Pull trailer PM](./pull-trailer-pm-checklist)
- [Scissor lift PM](./scissor-lift-pm-checklist)
- [Skid steer PM](./skid-steer-pm-checklist)

## EquipQR templates vs organization templates

The templates above ship with EquipQR as **EquipQR Templates** — best-in-class PM checklists for heavy equipment, machinery, and vehicles. You can **assign them to equipment directly** from the PM Templates page; cloning is only needed when you want to customize a copy for your organization.

## Apply a template to equipment in bulk

On **Operations → PM Templates**, every template card has an **Apply to Equipment** dropdown — the same multi-select picker used by daily check-ins:

1. The list shows equipment in your current organization, scoped by the team selected in the top bar.
2. Search, then use **Select all**, **Select none**, or **Inverse** to build your selection. Equipment already using the template as its default is marked **Current default**.
3. Click **Apply template** to set the template as the default PM on every selected record.
