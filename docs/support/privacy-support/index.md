# Privacy & Support

Report issues, track your tickets, submit a privacy request, and use the audit log.

## Articles

- [Submit a privacy request](./submit-privacy-request) — Request access, correction, deletion, or opt-out of your data, even if you do not have a signed-in account.
- [Use the audit log](./audit-log-basics) — Admins and Owners can see who did what and when — useful for investigations and compliance evidence.
- [DSR cockpit for privacy operators](./dsr-cockpit-overview) — Where Admins and Owners process data subject requests through verification, extension, completion, or denial.
- [Check system status](./system-status) — External uptime and incident monitoring for EquipQR. Always check here first if something looks broken.
- [Report a bug or issue](./report-an-issue) — Submit a ticket directly from the dashboard — session diagnostics attach automatically, no personal info required.
- [Track your reported issues](./track-my-tickets) — See ticket status and team responses under My Reported Issues on the dashboard Support page.
